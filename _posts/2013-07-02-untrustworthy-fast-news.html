--- title: "Untrustworthy Fast News" date: Tue Jul 02 2013 06:57:56 GMT+0100 (BST) tags: - journalism - slowness ---

Timeless words from a 19th century journalist on the use of the electrical telegraph:

Intelligence, thus hastily gathered and transmitted, has also its drawbacks, and is not so trustworthy as the news which starts later and travels slower.

Taken from The Information by James Gleick
