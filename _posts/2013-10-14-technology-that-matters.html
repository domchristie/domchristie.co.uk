I’m sure this won’t be the last time I mention Frank Chimero’s recent article, The Inferno of Independence; there are so many good parts, but here is just a snippet. It’s something I wanted to get across in my Why Hack? interview, but it’s articulated a lot better below:

The technology that matters only tries to change things by giving us more of what we’ve always wanted: autonomy, access, and community.

Frank Chimero in The Inferno of Independence

“Autonomy, access, and community.” Nailed it.
