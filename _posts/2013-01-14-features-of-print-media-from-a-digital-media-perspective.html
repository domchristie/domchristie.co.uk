--- title: "Features of Print Media (from a digital media perspective)" date: Mon Jan 14 2013 07:55:07 GMT+0000 (GMT) tags: - ---

This rather amusing list of print media features comes from Andrew Betts' talk, Offline Rules:

  • Works offline
  • Portable
  • Long battery life
  • Can be read in bright sunlight
  • Cheap
  • Ubiquity
  • Bookmarking
  • Sharing
  • Fast start up
  • Clipping/saving
