First, an interesting [idea from Pascal LalibertÃ© to combine Turbo Frames Turbo Streams](https://discuss.hotwired.dev/t/rails-turbo-frame-flash-and-tooltips/3788/2). Useful if you want update an area outside the Turbo Frame when the frame re-renders.

For example, let's say your Turbo Frame has a form, and after submission, you want to display a notice somewhere else, the response template might look something like:

```erb
<%= turbo_frame_tag :like do %>
  <%= render "form" %>
  <%= turbo_stream.update :notices, notice %>
<% end %>
```
