Here's [an Apple Shortcut (for iOS & macOS) that exports podcast subscriptions to an OPML file](https://www.icloud.com/shortcuts/5e49239698c44e92baf94399df86b3f9), making it easier to switch away from Apple's default Podcast app. It's based on the [Shortcut created by Pocket Casts](https://www.icloud.com/shortcuts/68ca7952a5374d4c8fa129e3db3798b9) (thanks!), but skips the importing part and instead just downloads the file.
